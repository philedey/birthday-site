The Plan

Your Jasper
Adventure

Date — TBD — Mark Your Calendar
01
8:00 AM

Maligne
Canyon

Start the morning in one of Jasper's most spectacular gorges. Walk bridges 1–4 as the canyon transitions from frozen winter to rushing spring melt — 50 metres of dramatic limestone walls carved over thousands of years. Early arrival means you'll have it nearly to yourselves.

✦ Tea house near the parking lot — perfect for a warm morning drink before you head in.
02
10:30 AM

Jasper
SkyTram

Ride Canada's longest and highest aerial tramway up Whistlers Mountain for jaw-dropping 360° panoramic views of Jasper, the Athabasca River valley, and peaks in every direction. There's a restaurant at the summit — a truly unforgettable spot for lunch above the clouds.

✦ Book tickets in advance at jasperskytram.com — Friday slots sell out quickly!
03
1:30 PM

Athabasca
Falls

One of the most powerful waterfalls in the entire Canadian Rockies — the sheer force of the glacial water carving through ancient quartzite is mesmerizing. A short, easy walk from the parking lot through a beautiful network of viewpoint platforms. Perfect for photos.

✦ In late spring/early summer the flow is at its most powerful. Expect a fine mist — the good kind.
04
4:30 PM

Miette
Hot Springs

The hottest natural hot springs in the entire Canadian Rockies — pools sit at a blissful 39°C. After a full day of adventuring, sink into warm mineral water surrounded by dramatic mountain peaks as the afternoon light turns golden. The 15km drive up Miette Road is stunning in itself.

✦ Check Parks Canada for seasonal opening dates. Bring towels and a dry bag.
05
7:00 PM

Jasper
Brewing Co.

End the most perfect day with a celebratory dinner at Jasper's beloved brewpub. Incredible burgers, fish & chips, giant Korean BBQ wings, and a lively atmosphere the whole family will love. A toast to 48 wonderful years — and many more adventures ahead.

✦ Call ahead to reserve a table — 780-852-4111. Don't skip the wings.

Good To Know

Before
You Go

🎫

Parks Canada Pass

You'll need a Parks Canada day pass to enter Jasper National Park. Buy online at reservation.pc.gc.ca to save time at the gate.

🛣️

Road Conditions

Check 511.alberta.ca before heading out — early morning roads in the Rockies can have icy patches depending on the season.

🧥

Dress in Layers

Temperatures in Jasper can swing 15°C through the day. Pack warm gear even if the morning looks mild — especially at elevation.

🐻

Wildlife

Keep eyes open along the Icefields Parkway and Miette Road — elk, bighorn sheep, and even bears are common sightings.

📱

Book Ahead

Both the Jasper SkyTram and Miette Hot Springs benefit from advance booking — especially on a Friday.

🎒

Pack a Snack Bag

Bring a packed lunch or snacks for the trail stops. Athabasca Falls is a beautiful spot to eat — and it saves time between activities.
